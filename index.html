Functionality

Recco

RECCO® is a two parts technology based on high penetration of ice and snow. This technology is used over 800 stations arround the world. We need an agreement to use this technology. That cost is included in our product price.

Thermal camera

We use thermal camera to ensure that we saw any kind of live above snow. It will also help us to find people with less than 50 cm of snow behind then.

A*

A* is a path finding algorithm use on many program such as gaming. Really low cost for a processor, we use it to know the best way to search on a determine area.

Computer driving

We use a computer embedded on rescue's bag. It will control automatic search for the drone and alert for rescuer if Boree find something. Also, he will be use to create a swarm of drone if many drone are in the place.

Rescuer's controller

Rescuer can also use drone as pilot mode to ensure security or to go on really difficult place such as crevasse or high peak.
